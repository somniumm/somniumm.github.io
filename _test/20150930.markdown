MX is an educational app (iOS + Android) that connects teachers and parents focusing on public primary schools in China. There are two versions of the app: one for teachers and one for parents. The platform allows teachers and parents to share moments of student's life whenever they are. Moments are automatically generated into a unique yearbook for each individual student. The app also contains a commuication tool for parents and teachers to reach eath other.
